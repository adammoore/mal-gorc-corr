About the Radial Visualization

This radial visualization provides an intuitive way to understand the complex relationships between MaLDReTH Research Data Lifecycle stages and the GORC infrastructure services that support them.

Visual Encoding:
  • Position: Distance from center indicates hierarchy (stages → categories → tools)
  • Color: Indicates correlation strength and service groupings
  • Size: Arc segments sized proportionally to importance
  • Connections: Curved lines show direct relationships between stages and services
Key Insights:
  • AAI and Helpdesk services connect to all 11 stages (universal support)
  • Research Object Repositories are critical for storage, preservation, and sharing stages
  • Direct Research Tools cluster around early lifecycle stages
  • Workflow services become important during processing and analysis phases
